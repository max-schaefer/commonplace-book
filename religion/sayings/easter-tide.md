# Nine Words for Easter Tide

1. _Peace be with you. As the Father has sent me, even so I am sending you._

   <div align="right">John 20:21</div>

2. _Blessed are those who have not seen and yet have believed._

   <div align="right">John 20:29</div>

3. _Touch me, and see. For a spirit does not have flesh and bones as you see that I have._

   <div align="right">Luke 24:39</div>

4. _Do not cling to me, for I have not yet ascended to the Father._

   <div align="right">John 20:17</div>

5. _If you forgive the sins of any, they are forgiven them; if you withhold forgiveness from any, it is withheld._

   <div align="right">John 20:23</div>

6. _All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me._

   <div align="right">Matthew 28:18</div>

7. _Behold, I am with you always, to the end of the age._

   <div align="right">Matthew 28:20</div>

8. _You will be my witnesses in Jerusalem and in all Judea and Samaria, and to the end of the earth._

   <div align="right">Acts 1:8</div>

9. _Stay in the city until you are clothed with power from on high._

   <div align="right">Luke 24:49</div>
