# Seven Last Words from the Cross

1. _Father, forgive them, for they know not what they do._

   <div align="right">Luke 23:34</div>

2. _Truly, I say to you, today you will be with me in paradise._

   <div align="right">Luke 23:43</div>

3. _Woman, behold your son! Behold, your mother!_

   <div align="right">John 19:26-27</div>

4. _My God, my God, why have you forsaken me?_

   <div align="right">Matthew 27:46</div>

5. _I thirst._

   <div align="right">John 19:28</div>

6. _It is finished._

   <div align="right">John 19:30</div>

7. _Father, into your hands I commit my spirit._

   <div align="right">Luke 23:46</div>
